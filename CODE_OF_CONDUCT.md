# Code of Conduct

FalconEye follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, be constructive, and assume good faith. Harassment, personal attacks, and discrimination are not tolerated.

Report concerns to security@osintph.info. All reports will be reviewed and acted on confidentially.
